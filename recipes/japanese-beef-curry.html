Japanese Beef Curry

Home

Description

A popular and easy to make dish from Japan. Served with chunks of cubed beef, potatoes and carrots.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chuck Roast
  • 4 Golden potatoes
  • 1 1/2 Sweet onion
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • thumb sized ginger - minced
  • 4-5 carrots - cut however you like
  • 1 pack of Golden Curry
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp apple/apricot jam
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 squares of dark chocolate
  • 2 tbsp of Worchershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp soysauce (maybe)
  • salt n peppa - as much as you like

Steps

  1. Season chuck roast with salt and pepper. Make sure you cover all sides. Put aside.
  2. Mince your garlic and ginger. Slice up your onion, carrots and potatoes however you like.
  3. Sear your chuck roast on all sides 4-5 minutes for each side. Set aside when done.
  4. Saute the onions until translucent, then saute your garlic and ginger until aromatic.
  5. Slide your beef cubes back in. Pour in chicken broth.
  6. Add your carrots and potatoes.
  7. If you are using a instant pot set it to high pressure for 20 minutes.
  8. Do a natural release up to 45 minutes and then release the rest of the pressure if it hasn't already.
